Who are we?

We are Carly Clayton and Jack Raybuck. We first met in Kansas in 2018, but moved to Phoenix in the summer of 2022. The two of us try to lead our lives with love and gravitate towards things that make us happy and bring sustainable and lasting joy. Jack's favorite things are art, design, & video games. Carly's favorite things are cooking, crafts, & our cat Ham.
