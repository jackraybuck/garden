Food

Clever Koi

Our favorite Ramen spot in Phoenix. Food and drink is always great.

Blanco

Our favorite Mexican restaurant in Phoenix. Not only is the food delicious, but the drinks are some of the best around. If I ever had to take a friend to one Mexican restaurant in the city, knowing I'd be able to get a seat, it'd be here.

Zipps

This is the only sports bar that matters in Phoenix. We've never been disappointed at the location in Midtown. Chicken fingers and wings are delicious, especially when ordered with one of their Desert Donkey Mules.

Parlor

Our favorite pizza place in the Phoenix/Scottsdale area. Carly loves the summer corn pizza.

Bad Jimmy's

The best burgers we've had in the city so far. It's a perfect balance of quality food and exciting atmosphere. Music is always great, they've got a fun bar and they stay open relatively late. Only drawback is that it's pretty pricey for a burger joint.

Glai Baan

Best Thai food in the Phoenix/Scottsdale area.

Culinary Dropout

Fun environment. Great outdoor seating when the weather is nice. Can be loud when it gets busy, but otherwise the food and drinks are great.

Welcome Diner

Very much a diner atmosphere. The only thing better than the food are the people who work here. The chicken biscuits are the best items on the menu without a doubt. Just make sure that you're prepared to either take some home with you or spend some time near the bathroom later.

Oregano's

If you want tasty Italian food with large serving sizes, and don't want to have to spend a lot of money, this is the place. You can never go wrong with the chicken parm and some of the garlic cheese bread.

Drinks

Central Records

If you get here around 5-6 pm it's usually pretty quiet, aside from the great music they've always got playing. Drinks are good, and they have a cool collection of records and books/zines.

Lola Coffee

Our go-to coffee shop in the Downtown Phoenix area. The people are friendly, the atmosphere is cozy, and the coffee (and butter croissants) are great.

Gracie's Tax Bar

Cheap drinks and appetizers (mostly chicken nuggets and tater tots). Fun, dive bar atmosphere. The first time we went they had Twilight playing at the bar. That should say it all.

Pigtails

Another place we feel obligated to take a visiting friend or family member. Cocktail bar located downtown that's tucked behind an unassuming oyster kitchen. The drinks are great, and we almost always order the cauliflower appetizer.

Wilderness DTPHX

Local brewery with great outdoor seating, right on Roosevelt near Downtown. When the weather is perfect this is one of the places to have a drink.

Eden Rooftop Bar

Easy-to-get-into rooftop bar right in the middle of downtown. Great view of the city, relatively affordable for drinks in that area.

Little O's

Near the C/A office off of McDowell. Fun patio area with good cocktails. The guacamole was good the one time we tried it.

McKenzie's Midtown Tavern

Close your eyes and try to imagine the bar that Matt Damon and Ben Affleck hang out at in Good Will Hunting. Open your eyes. You're inside Mckenzie's enjoying a drink at half the price of the next closest bar.

Bitter & Twisted

Fun cocktail bar in the middle of Downtown. It gives speakeasy vibes in the sense that it's hardly lit. The drinks are good, but as pricey as you'd imagine them to be Downtown.

Shopping

Changing Hands Bookstore

Locally owned bookstore with a cafe & bar tucked inside. Buy your books locally whenever you can!

Monsoon Market

Our favorite neighborhood shop in the Midtown area. They've got a great selection of beverages, from all sorts of natural wines to great tasting non-alocholic drinks.

Local Nomad

Another great small business in the Uptown area. Owned by some really cool folks, the shop sells a lot of thoughtful home goods.
